Job Positions

Hiring Pet Groomer

Job Requirements:

  • Love pets and have knowledge of pet grooming.
  • Possess relevant certificates and at least two years of grooming experience.
  • Good communication skills and teamwork spirit.
  • Capable of independently performing bathing, hair cutting, nail trimming, etc.
  • Patience and affection for animals, able to handle various breeds.

Salary:

Monthly Salary: ¥8000 - ¥12000 (based on experience and skills)

Hiring Pet Trainer

Job Requirements:

  • Passionate about animals, with professional pet training knowledge.
  • At least three years of pet training experience; certification preferred.
  • Able to observe animal behavior and create personalized training plans.
  • Excellent communication skills to effectively communicate with pet owners.
  • Teamwork spirit, able to coordinate work with other staff members.

Salary:

Monthly Salary: ¥10000 - ¥15000 (based on experience and skills)

Hiring Pet Caregiver

Job Requirements:

  • Passionate and patient with animals, able to provide basic care.
  • Experience in caregiving preferred; pet care certification is a plus.
  • Responsible for daily feeding, hygiene, and health monitoring.
  • Good communication skills to build trust with pet owners.
  • Able to adapt to a fast-paced work environment with flexible hours.

Salary:

Monthly Salary: ¥6000 - ¥9000 (based on experience and skills)
